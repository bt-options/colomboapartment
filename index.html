Features of Property

The two-story apartment complex has a fully furnished living room and dining area with a kitchenette. Enjoy the calm atmosphere of the in-door courtyard or lounge on the balcony. The building is designed and equipped to accommodate you with the most comfortable stay to ensure you feel at home.

  • FACILITIES

    The apartment has one master bedroom and the other has four bunk beds. Each room has an en-suit bathroom. Amenities include three wardrobes, iron, hairdryer, free toiletries and washing machine. The kitchen is furnished with pantry, oven, microwave, pop-up toaster, refrigerator, gas cooker and kitchenware.

  • MORDERNIZED APARTMENT

    Floor-length windows and doors surrounds the apartment for natural light to seep in. The interior has a contemporary furnishing with an ethnic appeal to it. The living area offers a flat screen TV with a satellite channel connection. Air conditioning and Wi-Fi is available.

  • HOUSE KEEPING

    House cleaning services and laundry press are available at an additional fee upon request.
